About Smart Result Calculator

Overview

Smart Result Calculator is an intelligent tool built to simplify the process of computing student performance in schools. It ensures accuracy, fairness, and speed in result compilation, following standardized assessment rules.

Features

  • Calculates Homework (20%), Test (30%), and Examination (50%) automatically.
  • Allows teachers to input their own comments (up to 3000 words).
  • Supports free and paid versions with flexible subject limits.
  • Export final results as professional PDF reports (Pro version).
  • Designed with simplicity, precision, and security in mind.

Purpose

The purpose of Smart Result Calculator is to provide educators with a dependable digital companion that eliminates manual stress, ensures fairness in grading, and promotes a smarter educational workflow.
